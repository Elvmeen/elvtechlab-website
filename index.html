WE'RE ASKED

How long does a project take?

Each project’s timeline depends on the scope. A simple landing page can be completed within 2 weeks while a full e-commerce platform might take 3 – 6 weeks. Once we understand your needs, well give you a clear, realistic timeline.

Can I manage the site myself after its built?

Definitely. Whether it’s updating content or adding new products, we can provide directional walkthrough guides that are easy to understand.

We also offer retainer plans if youd rather have us handle updates and ongoing support for you.

How does pricing work?

Well provide a custom quote based on your specific needs and project scope. If youre not making full payment upfront, a deposit is required before development begins. The remaining balance has to be paid before handover. You can check our pricing page to learn more

What do I need to get started?

All you need is to book a consultation and we will take it up from there.

Are your sites built with WordPress?

There are many great platforms available today and were flexible with what we use. We build sites using low-code tools like Framer, Webflow or WordPress and React or Next.js for fully custom builds

Once we understand your needs, well recommend the platform that best fits your project, timeline and budget.

I already have a website, can you work on it?

Yes, if you already have a website that needs improvements, we can help refresh the design and improve it’s overall functionality

Our site revamp service covers everything from minor redesigns to a complete site upgrade
